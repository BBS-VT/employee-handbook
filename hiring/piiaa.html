Proprietary Information and Inventions Assignment Agreement

Like with just about any other company, all employees will be required to sign a PIIAA contract. You'll be asked to do this on or before your start date. Loosely speaking, this type of document accomplishes two things:

  1. It requires you to keep our company's trade secrets confidential.
  2. It assigns ownership of work you create for our company to our company.
