Vacation Time

We want our employees to be happy and refreshed, so every employee will receive generous paid vacation time off for relaxation, adventures, time with loved ones, etc. This is in addition to other paid time off like paid personal leave and company observed holidays.

How it works

Your offer letter will state the amount of paid vacation time you receive each year. That amount is divided by the number of pay periods per year to determine what amount you earn each pay period.

For example, let's say you'll receive 80 hours (two full work weeks) of vacation time each year. Assuming a weekly pay period, you'd earn 1 2⁄13 hours of vacation time per pay period.

Accrued vacation time rolls over from year-to-year; however, your offer letter will specify a maximum amount of vacation time that you can have accumulated at any given time.

How to use

Vacation time off can be used by notifying the person you report to as early as reasonably possible and gaining approval. Under normal circumstances, at least a couple weeks notice is expected. Time off can be taken in half-hour increments.
